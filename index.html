Privacy Policy for SkiRack

Last updated: July 2026

SkiRack is a personal ski-testing app developed by Tommy Sørlien. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, SkiRack collects your email address for authentication purposes via Supabase. The app also stores data you enter about your skis, grind types, and test sessions, linked to your account.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: tracking and comparing ski test results. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party backend service. Data may also be stored locally on your device.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

Contact
